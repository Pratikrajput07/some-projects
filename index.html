About Our School

A Legacy of Learning Excellence

Established in 1985, Nirmala School has been at the forefront of holistic education, combining academic rigor with creative exploration and character development.

Our state-of-the-art facilities and experienced faculty create an environment where students can thrive academically while developing essential life skills.

With a student-teacher ratio of 15:1, we ensure personalized attention and tailored learning experiences for every child.
