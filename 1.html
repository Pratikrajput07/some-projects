About Our College

Discover our rich heritage and commitment to academic excellence

Established in 1965, GOVT EVPG College Korba has been a beacon of higher education in the region, fostering intellectual growth and character development for over five decades.

Our institution offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs across arts, science, and commerce faculties, supported by a team of highly qualified and dedicated faculty members.

The college is affiliated with Hemchand Yadav Vishwavidyalaya and recognized by UGC, ensuring the highest standards of education and research opportunities for our students.
